Services

How can I help you?

I work with anyone who has repetitive tasks — not just people who work with data.

Automating repetitive tasks

If you're doing the same thing every week — copying data, sending emails, updating spreadsheets — it can be automated. I help you identify exactly what and how, with tools that don't require permanent tech support.

AI tools for your workflow

I set up AI assistants that actually understand your context — your clients, your templates, your way of working. Think of it as a team member who's always available and never forgets.

Dashboards you'll actually use

One place to see what's happening in your business, updated automatically. No digging through files, no depending on someone to explain the numbers.

Simpler processes

Sometimes the answer isn't another tool — it's removing two you already have. I look at how you work and propose a leaner system: fewer steps, less friction, more clarity.

Real results

Before and after working together

⚡ Repetitive manual tasks

Before

Weekly reports built manually in Excel. Every Monday: copy, paste, fix errors, format. An hour and a half of work that adds no new decisions.

After

Automatically updated dashboard. The team reviews it in five minutes and knows exactly what decision to make.

📁 Scattered information

Before

Information scattered across forms, spreadsheets, and emails. Nobody knows which version is current. Coordinating takes more time than the actual work.

After

Centralized flow with a single source of truth. Fewer tools, fewer emails, less time searching.

🤖 Getting started with AI

Before

Ideas for using AI, but no idea if it makes sense, which tool to choose, or how to start without breaking what already works.

After

Functional prototype, clear criteria for next steps, and clarity on what's worth scaling — and what isn't.

🔍 Information monitoring

Before

Checking dozens of scattered sources every day. Hours lost searching for the same information, never sure if it was up to date.

After

Centralized dashboard that brings everything together in one place. What used to take days of monitoring now takes minutes to review.
